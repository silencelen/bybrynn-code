FAQ's

  • Mediums:
    You can find examples of pieces of mine in varying mediums in the “art” section of the “portfolio.” page. These include- gouache, watercolor, acrylic, & digital. I am open to working with other mediums as well! If you have something in mind not listed, feel free to suggest it.

  • Pricing:
    The price of commissions depend on the size of the piece. It may also vary depending on level of difficulty. Any framing & shipping costs will be additional prices.

  • Shipping:
    Currently, I am only able to ship within the US. (will update if/when International shipping is available as an option!)

  • Timing:
    The time it takes to create a piece, start to finish, varies (size, difficulty, amount of other projects I have/time I personally have available to work on it). When planning, it is best to keep in mind to allow at least a month, between discussing your piece & it being ready to ship. Please be patient! I understand wanting your piece as soon as possible, and while I will of course do my best, art takes time.
