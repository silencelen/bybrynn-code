Heart in My Hands

gouache on watercolor paper - 8x10in - 2023

“Here I am, heart in my hands.”
Throughout the process of this piece, from reference photos to sketch to painting, the end result ended up much softer than the initial concept. Instead of being a raw portrayal of a metaphorical act, it serves more as a depiction of the beauty in vulnerability.
It feels so right that this is my first real self portrait.
