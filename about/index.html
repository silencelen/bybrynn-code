Brynn Monahan

Artist & Creator

I'm a 23-year-old self taught artist based in Seattle, WA. My main medium is painting (usually with gouache), but I love to experiment with other materials from time to time (clay, stamp carving, digital, etc.). Currently, the majority of my portfolio is work inspired by media that is important to me. While I will always stand by "you are what you love" & wish to reflect that in my pieces; I'm working to move towards more personally significant & authentic pieces.

All the love,
Brynn
