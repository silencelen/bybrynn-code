" Slot Canyon, NV "

gouache on watercolor paper - 8x12in - 2022

Rocks & moon & sky.
I took the photo during a trip in Nevada earlier this month, and it has become my favorite photo I’ve ever taken. My attachment is a bit inexplicable, it’s not a particularly spectacular photograph, but nonetheless it’s mine and I love it. Naturally, I had to paint it as well. I hope y’all find the beauty in it as much as I do. Slot Canyon in Valley of Fire State Park, Nevada.
